**AniForge** is a personal anime tracker for Android — fast, offline, and built around how you actually watch anime.

No accounts. No cloud required. Just open the app and start tracking.

---

## What can you do with it?

### 📋 Track your anime
Organize everything you're watching into lists — **Watching**, **Completed**, **Plan to Watch**, **On Hold**, and **Dropped**. Update your episode progress with a tap and keep track of where you left off.

### 🔍 Browse the full catalog
Explore a built-in anime catalog that works completely offline. Filter by genre, tag, or studio to find exactly what you're looking for — no internet needed.

### 🌳 Follow franchises and sequels
Never lose track of a series again. AniForge maps out prequels, sequels, and spin-offs so you can see the full picture of a franchise in one place.

### 🏠 See what's going on at a glance
The home screen shows your recent activity, what you're currently watching, and quick shortcuts into your lists.

### 🖼️ Rich anime details
Every anime has a full detail page with cover art, synopsis, episode count, genres, tags, studio info, and more.
